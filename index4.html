The Mystery

About the Mystery: where do these two come from? Where is home? And, where are they going? Anywhere at all? If these questions bother you they inflict serious doubts into the daily existence of our hosts. All artist will tell you same: “it's not location, location, location. It's destination, destination, destination”. The end is the means. The rest is fluff. Only when the journey meets the final door will it all make sense. Once again into the path of the artist - maybe.

☜ Jump across this map here - maybe. Start with the birth place of the short one in the northern jungles of South America. Then bounce over to Europe and make sense of the many 'homes' that have been, that are, and that will become home: sweet. Not forgetting the roots of parents and home country but always looking for a new address to rest. Fill your passport pages - maybe.
